About

Twitter LinkedIn Github Scholar Morgan State

I’m a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Morgan State University’s Patuxent Environmental and Aquatic Research Laboratory (MSU PEARL).

My research interests are:

  • Ecosystem-based modeling of aquatic systems
  • Consumer-mediated nutrient transport modeling
  • Comparisons of ecosystem functioning across ecosystem borders
  • Trophic ecology of mesopelagic fishes
  • Network Analysis
  • Data visualization and visual inference

I am proficient in R (base + tidyverse), Primer, and Ecopath with Ecosim and have working knowledge of OSMOSE (Java-based), QGIS (Python-based) and Atlantis. I also have a rudimentary understanding of Bash, primarily for running models on servers. I’ve lived in the United States my entire life, but have bounced around Wisconsin, Minnesota, New Hampshire, Florida, and Maryland. My PhD, obtained at Florida International University in 2022, is in Biological Sciences with most of my code written in R. For the creation of this website, I also gained the experience of some front-end web development (HTML/CSS/JS).

I am a big advocate of open science and I try to deposit my code on GitHub. Please contact me for any code that you may like to utilize and/or any potential collaborations.
